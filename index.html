Capture Iowa is a campus photo project that showcases student life at the University of Iowa. At the end of the year, photos will be selected and shown in a photo gallery. Learn more or like us on Facebook!

Capture Iowa is a campus photo project that showcases life of first-year students at the University of Iowa. All first-year students are invited to submit photos.

No photography experience needed! Please share your photos from your experiences both inside and outside the classroom.

At the end of the year, photos will be selected and shown in the Class of 2020 Capture Iowa Gallery!

For more information please email captureiowa@uiowa.edu
