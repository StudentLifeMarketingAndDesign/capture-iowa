Capture Iowa is a campus photo project meant to showcase student life at the University of Iowa.

At the end of the year, photos will be selected and shown in a photo gallery in Calvin Hall.

Winners will be given options for a $20 or $40 gift card! Deadline to submit is April 4th.

The Class of 2017 Photo Gallery in Calvin Hall is meant to feature and highlight the experiences of first-year students at Iowa.

The Gallery Opening will be held on Monday, April 28th. The deadline to submit photos is also April 4th. For more information, please email captureiowa@uiowa.edu.
